    Web & Mobile Applications

    Web Site Design & Development, Responsive Mobile Apps for smartphones and tablets
    E-commerce platforms and ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system implementation.
    E-commerce implementation

    Inglobe implements an on line selling platform. The immediate benefits are to optimize the purchase process of regular customers, increase sales from wholesale customers, and expand the market to reach new direct customers, in Argentina and in the whole world.
    E-commerce implementation allows to:

    • Control sales via reports
    • Integrate e-commerce to the ERP of the company
    • Offer better funding opportunities for customers
    • Reduce human resources, maintenance and infraestructure costs, as compared to regular retail

    Odoo implementation
    Inglobe implements an ERP management Open Source system www.odoo.com
    The system is installed by modules, so it can be adapted to the company's structure.
    The most important modules are:
    • Messages
    • Sales
    • Purchases
    • Accounting
    • Stock
    • Manufacturing
    • Human Resources
    • Reports
    Web Pages

    A web page is a crucial presentation card for any company. It works according to the business’s needs, but all must be functional and dinamic, and must talk to all the publics of interest of the company.

    • Responsive sites for all mobile and tablet devices.
    • Web development within a few weeks.
    • Creative designs focused on specific audiences.
    A website can be either a simple institutional platform or a more complex development with catalogue, e-commerce, regular updates, self-management and other apps.

    Mobile Apps

    Mobile Apps can be process catalysts within the operations of a company, for employees or for customer relationships. Web apps can help streamline operation processes in many ways:

    • Ensuring data protection, uploading information to the web as soon as it is registered in the app.
    • Overcoming the difficulties of working from geographically distant points, sharing information.
    • Gathering information from multiple sources at the same time, for quick decision making.
    • As a control devise of processes status or collaborators.
